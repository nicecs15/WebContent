MODERNFORM GROUP
PUBLIC COMPANY LIMITED.

Established over 40 years ago, Modernform Group Public Company Limited has been the comprehensive furniture manufacturer and distributor, covering oce, home, kitchen furniture as well as the importer of furniture kits and home decors.

At Modernform, we believe that our mission is to develop the furniture which truly meets the modern lifestyle of the modern generation, to deliver full comfort and happiness through our specialized expertise, along with our always trending creation in the ever-changing world.



WHERE INNOVATION BECOMES LIVING

We are a leading provider of complete living furniture solutions, including home, oce, and healthcare, along with non-furniture solutions compromising fitting, architectural product, flooring, lift interior, and facade decoration.
